about vffm

About Victory Faith Foundation Ministries Victory Faith Foundation Ministries (VFFM) is a charity NGO based in Lugazi Namengo, dedicated to transforming lives through our motto "Faith in Action." We operate a school and church that serve as pillars of our comprehensive approach to community development, providing quality Christian education, supporting the needy and orphans, and offering youth discipleship and skills training programs. Our work extends throughout the community through compassionate outreach, practical assistance to vulnerable families, and vocational training that equips young people with both spiritual guidance and practical skills for sustainable livelihoods. We have built strong, lasting relationships with local families, leaders, and organizations, working collaboratively to identify needs and develop solutions that create lasting positive change. VFFM welcomes volunteers from all backgrounds who share our commitment to serving others, offering meaningful opportunities to contribute to our mission of empowering individuals and families through holistic development that addresses spiritual, educational, and practical needs. More than an organization, we are a family united by purpose, inviting everyone to join us in building stronger communities through faith, education, and service.

Expand Hope - Build a School for every child

At Victory Faith Foundation Ministries, we believe every child deserves a safe and inspiring place to learn. Currently, our school has only one permanent classroom block that serves the kindergarten section—Baby Class, Middle Class, and Top Class—while all other classes from Primary 1 to Primary 7 are taught in a temporary, makeshift structure. To change this, we are launching a special initiative to expand our school by purchasing more land and constructing permanent classroom blocks that can accommodate all our students. This expansion will significantly improve the learning environment and allow us to welcome even more children from the community who currently have no access to quality education. Your support will help us acquire land, build modern classrooms for Primary 1 through Primary 7, and fully equip them with desks, boards, and essential learning materials to create a space where every child can thrive.
