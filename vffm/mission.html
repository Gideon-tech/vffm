Mission causes

At Victory Faith Foundation Ministries, we are committed to transforming lives through faith-driven outreach and practical support. These are the key mission causes through which we serve our community in Lugazi, Uganda. You can choose to partner with us in any of these areas:

Child Sponsorship

Millions of children in Uganda are growing up without access to basic needs like shelter, food, education, parental care, health care and spiritual guidance. At Victory Faith Junior School, we enroll orphans and vulnerable children from low-income families, offering them not only a place to learn but also to grow in Christ.
